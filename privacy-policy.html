Privacy Policy for Oracle CPQ Admin Links

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

Oracle CPQ Admin Links ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles data.

Summary: This extension stores all data locally on your device, does not collect or transmit any personal information, and gives you complete control over your data.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

The Extension:

  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Does NOT track your browsing activity
  • Does NOT send data to external servers
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does NOT share data with third parties

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

What is Stored Locally:

  • Site Profiles: Your configured CPQ site names, URLs, and colors
  • Link Customizations: Your personalized link settings and overrides
  • Preferences: Dark mode setting and active site selection
  • Session Cache: Temporary session IDs for convenience (stored locally only)

Important Notes:

  • All data remains on your computer
  • Data is never transmitted over the internet
  • Data is not accessible to the extension developer
  • You can export, delete, or reset your data at any time

Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following permissions:

Storage Permission

  • Purpose: Save your site profiles and preferences locally
  • Scope: Limited to Chrome's local storage on your device
  • Data Access: Only data created by this extension

Tabs Permission

  • Purpose: Detect the current CPQ site URL for auto-switching feature
  • Scope: Read-only access to the active tab's URL
  • Usage: Only used to match hostname with your configured sites
  • Limitation: Does NOT read page content, history, or other sensitive data

Third-Party Services

Google Fonts (Optional)

The Extension loads the Roboto font from Google Fonts CDN for visual consistency. This is a standard web font service.

  • What's Shared: Your IP address (standard for any web request)
  • Google's Policy: Google Fonts Privacy Policy
  • Alternative: You can use the extension offline; system fonts will be used as fallback

No Other Third-Party Services

The Extension does NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • External APIs or databases

Data Security

  • All data is stored using Chrome's secure storage API
  • Data is encrypted by Chrome's built-in security mechanisms
  • No data transmission means no interception risk
  • You control all data through the extension's settings

Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

Export Your Data

Use the "Export Sites" button in settings to download all your configurations as a JSON file.

Delete Your Data

  • Delete Individual Sites: Use the "Delete Site" button
  • Reset All Data: Use the "Reset All Sites" button
  • Complete Removal: Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data

Data Portability

Export your configurations and import them on another device or browser.

Children's Privacy

This Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The Extension is designed for Oracle CPQ administrators and developers.
